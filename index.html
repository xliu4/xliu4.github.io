I am a PhD researcher in human-computer interaction at the Department of Computer Science at Aalborg University in Copenhagen, supervised by Carla Griggio and Niels van Berkel.

My work examines the sociotechnical dynamics of communication and collaboration around healthcare in online ecologies. I conduct empirical studies to evaluate how people navigate digital ecosystems and their constraints, and the adaptive strategies they develop to maintain (dis)communication within digital health contexts.

Prior to my PhD, I spent over six years in industry as a senior strategic design researcher, leading human-centered insight and strategy projects for global clients across tech, lifestyle, and beyond. I hold an MA in Intercultural Communication from the Intercultural Institute at Shanghai International Studies University, where I focused on computer-mediated communication and interpersonal dynamics.

I am always happy to chat about potential collaborations or shared research interests in HCI and CSCW. Feel free to reach out.
