I am a researcher and strategist focusing on understanding people and the cultural and technical contexts that shape them.

Currently, I am a PhD researcher in human-computer interaction at the Department of Computer Science at Aalborg University, supervised by Carla Griggio and Niels van Berkel.

My work examines the sociotechnical dynamics of communication and collaboration around healthcare in online ecologies. Through empirical studies, I evaluate how people navigate digital ecosystems and their constraints, and the adaptive strategies they develop to maintain (dis)communication within digital health contexts.
